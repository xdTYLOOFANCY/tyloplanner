🔔 Notifications & reminders

Free push notifications on your phone via ntfy: install the ntfy app (or open ntfy.sh), subscribe to a secret topic name of your choosing, and enter that topic here. You'll get a morning agenda (events + exam alerts) and an evening habit nudge.

🔄 Calendar auto-sync

Paste iCal URLs (one per line, e.g. your university timetable or Google Calendar secret address) and TyloPlanner re-imports them automatically in the background.

💾 Data

Your data lives in data/tyloplanner.db (SQLite) on your server. Use Backup/Restore in the header for JSON snapshots, or copy the .db file directly. Login credentials are set in .env (AUTH_USERNAME / AUTH_PASSWORD).
